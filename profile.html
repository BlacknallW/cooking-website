About Me

William Blacknall

"The key to being an effective teacher is being an effective learner"

Hello, my name is William and I am not good at taking pictures. This picture that you see here, which I am currently using across multiple platforms, is actually my best attempt out of easily 50 pictures with different expressions, lighting, and location. Now that you know just how much of a photagrapher I am not, let me tell you a little bit about who I am.

Weirdly enough, I don't really have a straight answer to that either. I used to be a teacher, but now I'm a (junior) developer. You know, the weird thing is I could go on and on about the content on the homepage, but I can honestly say the feeling of confusion I'm having while writing this "About Me" page is equivalent to going to a restaurant for the first time and having a notably impatient server. Anyway, read this next line like a Die Hard-esque action hero that just messed everything up by disobeying a direct order and going rogue on an important case.

"I let you down, chief. And I'll probably let you down again in the future. But don't you for a second think that it's because I don't care about you. You are more important to me than you'll ever know. You're forgiving, and that's exactly why I keep making mistakes, because I know you'll forgive me. But I've realized that what I actually want is to make you proud of something I've done right. Today is not that day, but tomorrow...tomorrow I'll be better."

Wasn't that fun? I'm glad we shared that moment together.
